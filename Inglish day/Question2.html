Reasons and purposes of the book

Reasons

Daniel Defoe wrote this book to combine his interest in avdenture and travel stories with a reflection on society and human nature, while capturing reader's attention.

His purposes were

Entertainment and commercial success, reflection on self-sufficiency and morality, commentary on society and economy, promotion of colonization and the spirit of adventure, and exploration of faith and providence.
