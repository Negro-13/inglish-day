How does this book compare to other books by the same author or in the same genre?

Daniel Defoe, unlike other authors of his time, uses a fantastic and realistic style in "Robinson Crusoe". It is one of the first adventure and realistic novels in English literature. Its first-person narration and focus on the development of the main character sets it apart from his works. This success created a new literary genre known as "La Robinsonada" , which deals with the survival of a castaway on a desert island.
