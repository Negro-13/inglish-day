Author of the author



Daniel Defoe

The author is Daniel Defoe, he was born in London around 1660 and died in 1731. He was on English writer,journalist,merchont, criminal and spy. His novel "Robinson Crusoe" was a success, making him a pioneer of the modern novel.

His background and experiences with the book are a combination of his activities in commerce and travel, which alloved him to vividly describe life on a deserted island. His journalist work helped him developed a clear a realistic style.
